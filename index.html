The Story
Built by a golfer, for golfers.

ShotSage started with a simple question: why do we all just guess out there? GPS watches tell you the distance. But they don't tell you what to do with it.

The app is bootstrapped, solo-built, and obsessively focused on one thing: making every golfer feel like they have a real caddie in their corner.
